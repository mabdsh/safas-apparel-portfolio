Our Manufacturing Process

6-Stage Quality Assurance

Precision Knitting

State-of-the-art computerized knitting machines creating seamless garments with consistent stitch density and tension control.

Eco-Friendly Dyeing

OEKO-TEX certified dye house using low-impact dyes with water recycling systems for sustainable coloration.

Laser Cutting

Computer-guided laser cutting ensures precision pattern matching and minimizes fabric waste.

Specialized Stitching

Overlock, flatlock, and coverstitch techniques executed by skilled technicians for durable seams.

Triple Quality Checks

In-process, pre-final, and final inspections at dedicated QC stations meeting AQL standards.

Smart Packaging

Anti-mold treatments, vacuum packing, and barcode tracking for efficient global distribution.
