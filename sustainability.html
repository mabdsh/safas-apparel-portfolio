Sustainability

Our Sustainability Vision

Our Vision

At Safas Apparel, we envision becoming a benchmark of sustainability, embodying the core values of significance, excellence, and institutionalization. We strive to lead the way in creating a greener environment and promoting social responsibility across the textile supply chain.

Our Approach

Through innovation and collaboration, we are transforming the textile industry towards a sustainable future. By embracing circular strategies, utilizing natural fibers, and practicing ethical principles, we weave sustainability into the very fabric of our business.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and maximizing social benefits. From responsible resource consumption to decarbonization efforts, we continuously strive for excellence — ensuring that sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. Safas Apparel has aligned its vision with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Standards & Ethics

Our Code of Conduct

Health, Safety & Environment

Safas Apparel operates in full compliance with all applicable laws, rules, and regulations, including those relating to worker health & safety and the environment.

No Discrimination

All employees are treated on the basis of their ability to do the job — regardless of gender, race, colour, language, nationality, ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political opinion, marital status, disability, age, or sexual orientation.

No Child Labour

Child labour is strictly prohibited. The minimum employment age at Safas Apparel is 18 years, with no exceptions.

No Forced Labour

Safas Apparel ensures there is no form of forced labour — including human trafficking, involuntary or bonded labour, or indentured labour of any kind.

Freedom of Association

Workers are free to join associations of their own choosing. Safas Apparel respects the right to lawfully and peacefully associate, organize, or bargain collectively.

No Harassment & Abuse

Safas Apparel treats all employees with dignity and respect, and does not tolerate bullying, harassment, intimidation, violence, or any form of physical or mental coercion.

Disciplinary Practices

Mutual respect is a core company principle. Any disrespectful or violent behavior — verbal or physical — is strictly prohibited across all levels of the organization.

Working Hours

Safas Apparel ensures all working hours comply with applicable laws and standards, and do not exceed the maximum limits permitted by law.

Wages & Benefits

Safas Apparel provides all legally mandated holidays, leaves, and benefits — including official holidays, annual leave, sick leave, maternity leave, social security, and old-age benefits.

Migrant Workers

Migrant workers enjoy the same rights and duties as local employees — covering remuneration, hiring, training access, and all other social criteria outlined in this Code of Conduct.
